Project 2

Exquisite Corpse

A collaboration between me and my classmate, Jordan Skube. The idea was to create a 3x3 grid of images to create one collage type image. We decided to try to make a landscape featuring mountains, the beach and sea, and a cityscape. My part was the sky above the sea and the shore (Hi again, little crab from Project 1!), the mountain’s middle, and the city’s towering buildings. The two of us decided to make a landscape because most other exquisite corpse pieces tend to make their artwork a living creature, such as a sort of alien, animal, or human. It was a quick project but despite this, I believe Jordan and I worked well together to create one semi-unified image.
